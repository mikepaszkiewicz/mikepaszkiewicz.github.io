Insurance

Through the years, many customers have asked us for help with their insurance as well. In response, we've created Blakefield Insurance Group, LLC, an insurance agency specializing in helping small to mid-sized businesses with property/casualty insurance and health insurance.  Blakefield has a unique focus on the hospitality and service industries.

The agents and brokers at Blakefield Insurance provide:

  • Group and Individual Health Insurance
  • Group and Individual Life Insurance
  • Property & Casualty Insurance (Personal Homeowners, Auto, and Umbrella Insurance)
  • Commercial Business Insurance (All Industries)
  • Workers Compensation
  • Disability Insurance

We think you'll find that Blakefield helps you get the most from your insurance dollar. In fact, Blakefield's goal is to save you money and help you retain employees. Blakefield Insurance is licensed in Maryland, Washington, D.C., Virginia, Pennsylvania, and Delaware. For more information, visit www.blakefieldinsurance.net.
