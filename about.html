Welcome to VLC

Vend Lease was founded in 1979 by Norbert and Raymond Paszkiewicz to provide equipment leasing to the vending industry throughout the Mid-Atlantic region. As we enter our fourth decade, Vend Lease has evolved into a premier financial services provider for all kinds of businesses throughout the United States.

About Us

Today, equipment suppliers continue to place trust in Vend Lease to handle the financing needs of their customers. They count on Vend Lease to consistently deliver the highest level of service and value within their respective industry.

As a direct funding source, we are well positioned to remain steadfast to the markets we serve, and we can remain competitive despite any economic or industry pitfalls that may occur.
