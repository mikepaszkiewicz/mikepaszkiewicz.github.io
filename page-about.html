Welcome to VLC

Vend Lease was founded in 1979 by Norbert and Raymond Paszkiewicz to provide
equipment leasing to the vending industry throughout the Mid-Atlantic region.
As we enter our fourth decade, Vend Lease has evolved into a premier financial services provider
for all kinds of businesses throughout the United States.
