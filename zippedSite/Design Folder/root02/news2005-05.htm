Bet You Didn't Know

Vend Lease allows easy "add-on" flexibility! If you find, after the initial sale, that your customer needs to add equipment, software, etc., our lease program provides an easy solution. Instead of having to execute another full lease, this "add-on" equipment can be wrapped into the existing lease with one easy document called a Lease Modification and it can be done at any time. So, the next time your customer needs additional equipment, software, etc. call us to see how easy this can be!

We offer Pre-Funding! We can pre-fund on any deal. Just let your sales rep know when you or your customer submits the credit application. Your pre-fund is sent out immediately upon return of fully executed lease documents and a verbal from the customer. Call your sales rep to learn more about our Pre-Fund program!
